About Us


AKGMA Social Club is a registered non-profit Socio-Cultural organization and certified as per the federal regulations in the UAE under Community Development Authority - CDA License No:66. AKGMA Social Club is the pre-eminent organization for Malayalees in Dubai region serving the community since 2011. This Club serves as the bedrock for all types of cultural, educational and social activities and to encourage and revitalize the traditional arts and crafts of Kerala.
